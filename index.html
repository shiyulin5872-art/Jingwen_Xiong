About Me

I am a passionate classical pianist currently pursuing a Bachelor of Classical Piano Performance at the Singapore Raffles Music College, expected to graduate in September 2026.

With a Grade 10 Piano certification and ABRSM Grade 8 Music Theory qualification, I combine technical proficiency with a deep understanding of musical expression. My academic focus includes Eastern and Western Music History, Music Management, New Media & Music, Creative Project Management, Music Pedagogy, and Instrumental Writing.

I have extensive experience as a piano educator, event organizer, and content creator, with a proven track record of student success in examinations and competitions. Fluent in both English and Mandarin, I bring a cross-cultural perspective to my music and teaching.
